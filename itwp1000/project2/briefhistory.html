A Breif History of the NFL

The NFL was established in 1920. Originally the season champion was the team with the best win loss record usually shown 12-4 or 15-1 with the wins in the front and the losses in the back. In 1933 a playoff system replaced the old method for picking a season champion. 34 years later in 1967 the NFL merged with the AFL (American football league) thus began the tradition of the Super bowl. 2 divisions and only 1 league champion.

Since 1970 a new trophy is issued each year for the winner of that season to keep. The trophy was named the Vince Lombardi Trophy after a head coach who led the Green Bay Packers to victories in the first two Super bowls. The trophies are produced by Tiffany & Co. and made from sterling silver It’s also said that the trophy’s worth ranges between $25,000-$300,000. The winner of each conference also receives a trophy. For the AFC, American Football Conference, the trophy is the Lamar Hunt Trophy and the NFC, National Football Conference, the George Salas Trophy.
