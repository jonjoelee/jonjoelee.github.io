Winners!


Here are our winners! If you found your photo upon arriving here, congratulations! The community decided that your photo was the best for it's designated category! If you we're hoping yours is here and its not, make another submission! never give up! For now, let's celebrate the current winners as they deserve it! If you didn't make the cut, again remember that in order for one person to be declared the winner several of us must place second and eventually through facing enough failure you will succeed. Also remember we have 3 main categories and if your photo doesn't fall into one of those three it ends up in the Miscellaneous category and thus will likely have more competition.


If you're curious how close you were from being awarded one of our winners feel free to reach out and we can discuss your analytics via email. If you'd like us to implement another system for determining a winner feel free to contact us at the bottom of the page and make a suggestion for more creative, diverse ways for determining who's photos get showcased under the winner tab.
