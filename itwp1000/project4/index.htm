Welcome to the Portraits of Humanity!


Grateful and excited you made it here! At Portraits of Humanity our goal is to share photography and facilitate an environment where people can share photos with one another, vote on them, and converse in our chatroom. Each month we pick a winner from each of our three categories and display their photo on the winners page. If you'd like to make a submission please do! Be sure to read our rules page first then navigate on over to the submission page. Don't forget to vote for others under the vote tab as community decision is how we determine the winner for each month.


Feeling bored and already made your own photo submission? Hop over to the Chatroom! Leave a question or comment if no one is there and maybe when you return to the site someone will have left a thoughtful reply. If there are any questions or concerns related to the site whether its about functionality submissions or something else feel free to contact me with the clickable link at the bottom of each web page titled 'Click here to send me a personal email'. Thank you again for visiting Portraits of Humanity, I hope today you find inspiration, motivation, and peace.
